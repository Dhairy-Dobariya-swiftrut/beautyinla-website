How btl eMsella Works

The pelvic floor muscles are crucial in supporting pelvic organs and maintaining continence. However, the natural aging process, childbirth, and menopause can weaken these muscles, resulting in issues like urine leakage when coughing or sneezing.

BTL EMSELLA employs High-Intensity Focused ElectroMagnetic Energy (HIFEM) to stimulate and strengthen all pelvic floor muscles, going beyond what can be achieved with voluntary contractions (such as Kegel exercises). This comprehensive approach ensures that even the deeper forces are engaged, effectively restoring continence and the confidence to partake in daily activities without fearing bladder control issues.

Here's how it works

1. Each 28-minute session involves sitting comfortably on the EMSELLA chair while it generates thousands of supramaximal pelvic floor muscle contractions.

2. Remarkably, each session is equivalent to performing 11,000 pelvic floor exercises, all without the effort typically associated with manual exercises.

3. The procedure is entirely non-invasive, and you remain fully clothed throughout.

4. No medication is required, and any sensation during the treatment is typically minimal, often described as a slight tingling.

5. There is no need for recovery time; you can resume your regular daily activities immediately after each session.

To achieve optimal results, most individuals typically undergo a course of six sessions over a three-week period. Many people begin to notice significant improvements in as few as two or three treatment sessions.
