Advanced Body Remodeling

A safe and comfortable non-surgical solution to refine and reverse skin laxity.

How Many Sessions Are Required?

Plus uses fractionated radio frequency energy to stimulate collagen production for body remodeling. After Plus treatments you can achieve a toned skin contour making your body appear smoother and younger.

Is Plus For Me?

Plus is recommended for individuals who are looking for a non-invasive skin treatment to address targeted areas that require contraction. It is safe and can be used on all skin types and tones.

What Results Will I See And Feel?

During treatment most patients will feel a heat sensation and “feel” tighter. Over time, patients typically notice a reduction in the folds of their skin leading to a smoother contour of the body. Textural irregularities will even out, and sagging skin will appear more toned.

How Does Plus Work?

Plus was created based on the clinical success of the proprietary A.C.E. (Acquire, Control, Extend) technology. It targets deep within the skin to ensure that no areas are under-treated.

What Areas Can Be Treated?

Plus can be used on parts of the body that may need contraction after the effects of weight gain/loss or aging. Common areas include the abdomen, arms (bat wings), thighs, back fat, and knees.

Will It Hurt?

Plus is painless and has no downtime; it feels like a hot stone massage. Patients may see slight redness in the treated area, which subsides within a few hours.

How Many Sessions Are Required?

Weekly sessions over six to eight weeks are recommended. Mild cases may need fewer sessions. Scheduling is based on a pre-evaluation, and best results are noticed over time or after a series of treatments.
