Who Can Benefit from This Treatment?

BTL EMSELLA treatment is suitable for individuals facing various pelvic floor-related conditions, including:

  • Stress incontinence resulting from menopause or childbirth.
  • Embarrassing urine leakage when laughing, coughing, sneezing, or during exercise.

  • Intimate health concerns such as vaginal laxity and challenges reaching orgasm.

This treatment is effective for both men and women, addressing bladder control and intimate health concerns.
