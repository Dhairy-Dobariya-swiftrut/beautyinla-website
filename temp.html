What Is MorpheuS8?

Morpheus8 is a sub-dermal adipose remodeling device (SARD) that stimulates collagen production of the underlying layers of the dermis. By targeting the deeper layers of the skin, the building blocks will reorganize themselves in a natural anti-aging process.
